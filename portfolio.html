恋上大腹黑/Fall in love with a big Sly

This comic follows a kind-hearted and unsuspecting protagonist who, after accidentally traveling back in time to ancient China, finds herself serving as a painter in the imperial palace. Unbeknownst to her, she becomes entangled in the power struggles and intrigues among the princes of the royal court.

《动漫作品赏析：融媒体的视域》/Analysis of Anime Works: A Multimodal Perspective

编者：陈骞、陈芷明/Editors: Qian Chen, Zhiming Chen

Possibly the first book in the nation (or even the world) -- A handbook-style textbook written for all future media professionals, drawing universal media experience from the "second dimension" (anime culture) A strategy guide that starts from the anime media of the current "post-animation" era, offering solutions for the symbiosis of anime content and media technology forms An engaging textbook that delves into the imaginative power of anime through the relationship between text and images, making complex concepts accessible Coming soon... in 2025
